Sublimation Paper

At Shree Bhawani Paper Mills, we craft premium Sublimation Paper – engineered for vibrant, high-definition transfers onto polyester fabrics and hard surfaces. Our sublimation paper ensures exceptional color vibrancy, sharp details, and long-lasting results for a wide range of applications.

Key Features:

  • GSM Options - Available in 45 GSM to 70 GSM to accommodate different printing and transfer needs.
  • Fast Drying - Our specialized coating allows for quick ink drying, minimizing smudging and maximizing production efficiency.
  • High Ink Release - Formulated for optimal ink transfer, delivering rich, saturated colors onto your chosen substrate.
  • Consistent Quality - Manufactured to exacting standards, ensuring consistent performance and reliable results every time.
  • Compatible with Various Inks - Designed for use with a variety of sublimation inks.

Applications:

  • Apparel - Ideal for custom t-shirts, sportswear, fashion apparel, and personalized clothing.
  • Home Decor - Perfect for creating personalized cushions, blankets, mugs, and other decorative items.
  • Promotional Products - Excellent for customizing promotional gifts, such as keychains, phone cases, and lanyards.
  • Signage & Displays - Suitable for producing vibrant and durable signage for indoor and outdoor use.
  • Personalized Gifts - Create unique and memorable gifts for any occasion.

Whether you're creating custom apparel, personalized gifts, or eye-catching promotional items, our Sublimation Paper guarantees vibrant colors, sharp details, and lasting impressions.
